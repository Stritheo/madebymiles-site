About Miles

A short overview you can keep up to date as your roles and focus evolve.

I work with Boards and executives to formulate and deliver strategies that transform insurers by improving the customer experience at a lower unit cost.

Over the past two decades I’ve led teams of up to 700 people through mergers, divestments, platform migrations and regulatory reforms across Suncorp, Westpac, Commonwealth Bank, Hollard and Strata Community Insurance.

I lead with accountability, care and purpose to create lasting value through performance, trust and delivering customer outcomes. Lately my focus has been on using data and agentic AI to unlock better decisions for customers, communities and insurers.

Outside work I’m a husband, dad, learner and maker – usually experimenting with AI, sketching new product ideas or planning the next family adventure.
